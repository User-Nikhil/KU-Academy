  Message From The Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Guardians,

With heartfelt warmth, I extend a gracious welcome to K.U. Academy, an educational sanctuary nestled in the very heart of eastern Nepal. Over the years, we have forged a legacy, steeped in a distinguished history of academic excellence. This heritage, dear to our hearts, serves as a testament to our enduring commitment to nurturing the unique potential residing within each individual, while simultaneously fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment.

Within the hallowed halls of K.U. Academy, we are driven by an unwavering devotion to providing our students with an education that is nothing short of comprehensive and well-rounded. Our team of devoted educators, fired by a passion for teaching, works tirelessly to ensure that our students have access to the very best. Backed by state-of-the-art facilities, our classrooms resonate with the excitement of discovery, while our extensive library serves as a repository of knowledge, beckoning young minds to explore the boundless realms of literature.

It is our firm belief that every student, under our guiding wings, possesses the potential to soar to great heights. We encourage our students to dream big, to cultivate the invaluable virtues of diligence and determination, and to embark upon a lifelong journey of learning. It is their resolve, synergized with the active participation of our dedicated parents, that forms the bedrock of our mission – to craft an environment where each and every individual can flourish and ultimately attain their full potential.

As we venture forth into yet another academic year, our hearts are brimming with anticipation for the wondrous possibilities that lie ahead. In the classrooms, on the playing fields, and within the hearts of our students, we envision boundless opportunities for learning, personal growth, and remarkable achievements. Together, hand in hand, as one cohesive community, we shall carve out a future that is resplendent with the echoes of success, under the benevolent umbrella of K.U. Academy.

Warm regards,
Samir Subedi
Principal, K.U. Academy
