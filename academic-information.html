Academic Information
Home    Academic information

Welcome to K.U. Academy, where education is a journey of growth and discovery. Our academic structure is designed to provide a well-rounded and flexible learning experience that caters to the diverse needs and aspirations of our students. Here's a closer look at our academic programs:


Academic Structure at K.U. Academy:


Early Childhood Education (ECE):

The Early Childhood Education section includes Pre-Nursery, Nursery, LKG (Lower Kindergarten), and UKG (Upper Kindergarten) classes. In this nurturing environment, our youngest learners embark on their educational journey. The curriculum emphasizes the development of foundational skills and includes subjects such as English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, General Knowledge, Health Education, and Vocational Studies.

Primary and Secondary Levels:

From Grade 1 to Grade 5, students continue their academic journey with a focus on building a strong educational foundation. Subjects taught during these years include English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, General Knowledge, Health Education, and Vocational Studies. From Grade 6 to Grade 10, students further develop their academic skills with a comprehensive curriculum covering English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, General Knowledge, Health Education, and Vocational Studies. Additionally, from Grade 6 onwards, students have the option to delve into specialized subjects such as Accounting, Computer Science, and Advanced Mathematics. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education, preparing them for higher levels of learning.

National Examination Board (NEB) Affiliation:

Our school is proud to be affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB). Students are prepared for the NEB examinations, ensuring that they meet national educational standards and are well-equipped for future academic pursuits.

Grade 11 and 12 - Education and Management Faculty:

In Grade 11 and 12, students have the opportunity to specialize in the fields of Education and Management. The curriculum is carefully crafted to provide in-depth knowledge and skills in these areas. Subjects include English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science, and specialized subjects related to the chosen faculty. This phase prepares students for higher education and future careers in the fields of education or management.

At K.U. Academy, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and flexible educational experience that caters to the diverse needs and aspirations of our students, preparing them for success in their academic journeys and beyond.
